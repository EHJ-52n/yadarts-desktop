These folder allows the inclusion of additional themes in respective sub-directories.

A theme shall provide the following files:

mytheme/background.jpg - a general background image; 1 pixel is sufficient
mytheme/background_alt.jpg - a background image used in content areas
mytheme/board-hi.jpg - a 1500x1500 pixel sized image of the dart board
mytheme/board-m.jpg - a 1000x1000 pixel sized image of the dart board
mytheme/board-lo.jpg - a 750x750 pixel sized image of the dart board
mytheme/corner_topleft.jpg - image used as the corner of borders around content areas
mytheme/border_left.jpg - image used for borders around content areas

Optional files:
mytheme/theme.properties - a meta description of the theme (see theme.properties.template)
